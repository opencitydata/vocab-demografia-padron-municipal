Introduction back to ToC

After the development of the Vocabulary for the data representation of the [census of inhabitants]() (census microdata or raw data), different cubes have been defined in order to facilitate its statistical exploitation. Therefore, in this vocabulary the cubes' structures to represent the aggregated data of the census data (census macrodata) are presented. The cubes are defined by means of the concepts and properties described in the [W3C Data Cube]() vocabulary. Specifically, there have been generated the instances that gather the essential details to represent the structure of each cube. Likewise, to represent the dimensions and measures required, different vocabularies and code lists have been reused and defined. This vocabulary has been developed in the context of the project "Plataforma de gobierno abierto, colaborativa e interoperable" (Open Government collaborative and interoperable platform), where the municipalities of Madrid, Zaragoza, Santiago de Compostela and A Coru√±a are involved.
